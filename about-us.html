Welcome to the Aujan Industrial Supplies (AIS)

.

We are proud to present what we believe is the best selection of health and safety products in the industry.

Since 1977, AIS has been protecting the heart of the oil and gas, chemical industry with the highest quality safety products and solutions. We serve all segments of the market: construction, police, civil defense, medical, manufacturing and government. Through our four branches we provide quality service and branded products.

Every business, from the smallest shop to the largest industrial plant, needs protection for their employees and its facility. AIS carries products and services from such trusted names as MSA, Honeywell, 3M, Brady, and many international brands to create unparalleled fire and safety protection.

At AIS, we understand that workplace health and safety is foundational for success in any business. These pages are full of products that are NFPA (National Fire Protection Association) and OSHA (Occupational Safety and Health Administration) standards to keep operations running according to internationally respected health and safety measures.

Our people know safety, and we can deploy it for your business. AIS provides expert teams to handle turn-key projects,takinga job from the design phase all the way through commissioning
